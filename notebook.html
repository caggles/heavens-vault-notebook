Use the buttons on the left to navigate between pages. Do not use the back button on your browser.

Use the save and load buttons above to save your vocabulary notebook between sessions. There is an autosave - your vocabulary list will remain between sessions, even if you close your browser. However, it doesn't work in older browsers. Even if the autosave works for you, you should still manually save often to protect against problems like a cleared cache. Further instructions on saving/loading can be found by clicking the buttons.

Click on the ancient symbols keyboard above to create words. As you add more symbols, it will check your existing vocabulary and show you all words that it believes are related. Below, you will see possible translations of the new word which have been formed by stringing together your existing translations for the words and symbols that make up this word. You can mouse over these "subwords" to see the ancient verion of each one.

Once you have typed out the word, add your guess for a translation in the box, and click "Add Word to Vocabulary." The word will be added to your vocab list for you to view, and to be used later as an aid in other translations. You may add multiple translations of the same ancient word by adding it multiple times to your vocabulary, each with a different translation.

If you make a mistake while entering your ancient word, click "Clear" to start again.

Below, you will see your full vocabulary (if you have one)! Click on any ancient word in this list to view more detailed information on your translation, as well as similar words in the vocabulary list.

This page displays information about your translation of a particular ancient word.

In the "Manual Translations" section, you will see your own translations for the word. You can have multiple translations for a single word. There are often multiple correct translations for the same word, and this can also be used to record multiple unrelated guesses at a translation while you decide which one is correct. You can delete any one of your translations by clicking the "delete" button next to it. Note that you cannot delete a translation if it is the only one you have for a word. You must add at least a second translation to delete one of them. You can add a second translation by returning to the "Add A New Vocab Word" screen and adding the same ancient word again, with a new translation.

Below the translations, you will see lists of related words. Clicking on any of the ancient words will take you to the detailed translation information for that word.

Here, you can enter your guesses for the meanings of each individual symbol. As you record these, they will show up as part of the symbolic translations on the other pages, and when you add a new word to your vocabulary.

To update them, simply type your translation into the box next to the symbol, and then click "Save Symbol Translations." Note that this is NOT the same as saving your vocabulary notebook as a whole.
